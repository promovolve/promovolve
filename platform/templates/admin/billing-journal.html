{{/* What the selected type actually does — the double-entry in words. */}}

Credit — goodwill at the platform's expense: the account gains the amount, platform revenue gives it up. No real cash moves.

Charge — the mirror of a credit: the account loses the amount, platform revenue takes it back. For clawing back an over-credit.

Refund — advertisers only: their wallet and platform cash both go down — real money leaves the books, and you make the actual transfer back yourself.
