{{/* ----- How each floor is set (the honest hybrid) ----- */}}

How each floor is set

  • One bidder in a category → the floor is set straight to that bid. With a single advertiser, their bid is the revenue-optimal reserve, so there's nothing to measure — they pay full value, and the floor follows a bid change instantly.
  • Two or more bidders → a sweep tries candidate floors against real revenue and keeps the one that earns most. (The site-wide sweep below shows this mechanism in action.)
  • Too little traffic → the floor holds at your minimum rather than drift up on noise.
